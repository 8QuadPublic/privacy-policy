8Quad Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24 Sept 2024

8Shop is a business-to-business (B2B) ecommerce platform that connects buyers and sellers on the Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use the 8Shop app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information:

  • User Details: Name, phone number, account type (Retailer, Distributor, or Manufacturer), location, and address.
  • Business Details: Know Your Customer (KYC) information, business location, and product catalog information (for sellers).
  • In-App Activity: Data on your interactions and activities within the app.
  • Order Details:
    • Product details (name, quantity, and specifications).
    • Order timelines (order date, shipping date, and expected delivery date).
    • Payment information, billing address, and shipping address.
  • Uploaded Images: Any images you choose to upload to the platform.
  • Device Information: Device ID and related technical data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used for the following purposes:

  • To communicate with you.
  • To personalize and improve your experience on the platform.
  • To facilitate order fulfillment and transaction processing.
  • To provide you with information or advertising related to our products and services, in line with your preferences.
  • To enhance and improve our products and services.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information under the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Your device ID is shared with Exponent (a React Native development tool) to enable push notification services.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, such as responding to subpoenas, court orders, or other lawful requests.
